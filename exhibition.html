Currently On Display

Deities of Nepal

( May 5 - May 25 )

Deities of Nepal is a curated art exhibition that brings together rare and remarkable depictions of divine figures from Nepal's rich religious heritage. Featuring both ancient masterpieces and contemporary works created in traditional styles, the exhibition offers a timeless journey through the sacred iconography of Hindu and Buddhist deities. Each artwork reflects the spiritual, cultural, and artistic legacy passed down through generations, preserving intricate techniques and symbolism. From the serene gaze of Avalokiteshvara to the powerful form of Bhairab, the exhibition invites viewers to witness how devotion, mythology, and craftsmanship continue to shape Nepal's artistic soul.
