Welcome to Qala

Art gallery devoted to the spirit of Nepali culture and community.

We celebrate art that carries memory, movement, and meaning — work rooted in tradition yet alive with the present. At Qala, each exhibition is a conversation between the past and the possible, showcasing artists who reflect the richness of Nepal's visual language and the complexities of its changing identity. This is a space where heritage is honored, stories are shared, and creativity is community.

Currently On Display

Deities of Nepal (May 9 - May 29)
Highlights

Lalit, The Buddha Protector

Avalokiteshvara

Lokeshvara Consort Vishvamata

Manjushree Teaching of Buddha

Deities of Nepal is a curated art exhibition that brings together rare and remarkable depictions of divine figures from Nepal's historic heritage. Featured icons mirror the magnificence and contemporary relevance of Buddhist, Hindu, and Tantric traditions. From ancient masterpieces to modern interpretations, discover detailed artworks, sacred objects, and paintings that reveal Nepal's spiritual and artistic evolution. The exhibition explores the symbolism, rituals, and stories behind each deity, offering visitors a unique opportunity to experience the powerful form of bhakti, the relationship between the divine and the devotee, and the rich, mythology and craftsmanship shaping Nepal's culture.
